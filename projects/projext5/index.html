Apart from the norm of scenarios You might encounter, at creative stage, as a basis for web-page to contain images, and you've been preparing them from 'ground up' (i.e. right after getting img's prepared). Scenario(1): actual Copying of Files to Project's dir [App You've been using, Paint.NET/Gimp/Krita/ to edit img-file, and it has the img-file stored in PC's dir or Cloud (i.e. from online source may require to Dwnld&Save it as 'local file') ].

  • In this scenario: move the file from locally stored w/command applied in terminal.
  • There is a command (available in unix/Linux): mv [filename] [to-a-folder's dir-path].
  • In Linux: moves & renames that img-file (both at-the-same-time).

And, using mv command is practical in such ToDo's that are completely outside of the Web Browser environment. What alternative scenarios You might come across ?
